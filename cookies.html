Cookie Policy

With this cookie policy, the website operator informs the website to the user about the use of cookies or similar storage technologies on this website (hereinafter "cookies").

What is Cookie?

Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device by the web browser to store certain information. The next time you visit our website with the same device, the information stored in the cookies is sent to our website ("First Party Cookie") or another website to which the cookie belongs ("Third Party Cookie").

Through the information stored and returned in the cookie, the relevant website recognizes that the user has already accessed and visited the end device with the web browser. We use this information to display the website to its users in the most appropriate way according to their preferences.

However, only the cookie itself is defined on the end device. The further storage of personal data takes place only if the user gives us his express consent or if this storage is absolutely necessary in order to use the service offered and called.

Approving the use of cookies

Cookies that are not strictly necessary to provide services on this website are used only after giving your consent. The user accepts the use of cookies by allowing the use of cookies on the website based on a notice given by us ("cookie title").

The user can adjust the cookie settings at any time, for example by enabling or disabling individual cookie categories.

How the user can disable or delete cookies in general in his browser (including strictly necessary cookies) is described in the section "Disabling or deleting all cookies".

Legal basis

If the user has consented to the use of cookies on the website based on a notice given by us ("cookie strip"), the legality of the data processing is based on Article 6 (1) sentence 1. A GDPR. If no consent is requested, 6th paragraph 1 sentence 1 article GDPR is the legal basis for the use of cookies.

Used cookie categories

Depending on the purpose and function, we categorize the cookies used by the website into the following categories:

Strictly necessary cookies

Functional cookies

Performance cookies

Marketing / third party / consent cookies

Definitely necessary cookies

Definitely necessary cookies keep this website or service working properly. This means that without these cookies, this website or service cannot be used as intended. These types of cookies are used only by the operator of the website (first party cookies) and all information stored in the cookies is only sent to this website.

Definitely necessary cookies cannot be separately disabled or enabled. The user has the option to change his own cookie settings at any time or to disable cookies in general in his browser (see "Disable or delete all cookies").

Functional cookies

Functional cookies enable this website to provide information such as: B. saving the username or language choice and providing the user with enhanced and personalized functions accordingly. These cookies only collect and store anonymized information.

Functional cookies are not used on our website.

The user can always object to the use of functional cookies by adjusting the cookie settings accordingly.

Performance cookies

Performance cookies collect information about how our websites are used to improve their attractiveness, content and functionality. These cookies, for example, help us determine whether subpages of our website are visited and which subpages are particular interest. We record the number of visits to a page, the number of accessed pages, the spent time on our website, the order of the visited pages, the search terms our users search for us, the country, the region and the geography.
